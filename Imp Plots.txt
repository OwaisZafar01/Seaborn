

Master just these 7-8 graphs, cover 90% of the work in data science/ML/AI field:

Top 8 Must-Master Graphs

Matplotlib - Core Essential Plots

Line Plot - Time series data, trends dikhane ke liye
Bar Plot - Categorical data comparison
Histogram - Data distribution dekhne ke liye
Scatter Plot - Two variables ka relationship
Subplot - Multiple plots ek saath dikhane ke liye

Seaborn - Must-Know Plots

Heatmap - Correlation matrix, confusion matrix ke liye
Boxplot - Outliers aur data distribution
Countplot - Categorical variables ki frequency
Distplot/Histplot - Data distribution with density curve
Pairplot - Multiple variables ka relationship ek saath
Barplot - Categorical data with error bars

Most Critical Ones (Top Priority)

Histogram - Har dataset mein pehle ye dekhte hain
Scatter Plot - Feature relationships ke liye
Heatmap - Correlation analysis ke liye (bahut important!)
Boxplot - Outlier detection ke liye
Line Plot - Time series analysis ke liye
